## Section 1: This Sunday's Service (5 minutes)

Let's look at how you'd prepare for Sunday's worship service.

### Step 1: View the Bulletin

1. Click **"Bulletins"** in the top menu
2. You should see a bulletin for "next Sunday"
3. Click on that bulletin to open it

**What to check:**
- Can you see the order of service?
- Does it show songs, sermon, prayer, offering, etc.?
- Look at the **Sermon** item in the list

### Step 2: Linked Sermon

1. Find the Sermon item (should say something like "Pressing On Toward the Goal")
2. Click the sermon title or any "View Sermon" link

**What to check:**
- Does it take you to the sermon details page?
- Can you see the sermon manuscript?
- Are the scripture references visible?

---

## Section 2: Sermon Archive (10 minutes)

Now let's explore the sermon library.

### Step 3: Browse Sermons

1. Click **"Sermons"** in the top menu
2. You should see a list of past sermons

**What to check:**
- Do you see multiple sermons listed?
- Can you tell which series each sermon belongs to?
- Look for the "Philippians: Joy in Chains" series

### Step 4: Filter Sermons

1. Try using the **filters** at the top:
   - Set the date range to "last 30 days"
   - Or filter by preacher name (try "Pastor John Smith")
2. Click "Apply Filters" or wait for results to update

**What to check:**
- Do the results change based on your filter?
- Can you reset the filters easily?

### Step 5: View a Sermon in Detail

1. Click on **"Joy in the Gospel"** (or any sermon that catches your eye)
2. Scroll through the details

**What to check:**
- Is the full manuscript readable?
- Can you see the primary scripture reference?
- Are there any audio or video links? (They might be example URLs)

### Step 6: Edit a Sermon

1. On the same sermon detail page, click **"Edit Sermon"**
2. Make a small change to the manuscript (add a sentence or fix a typo)
3. Click **"Save Sermon"**

**What to check:**
- Did the edit save successfully?
- Does the page show your updated text?
- Was it easy to find the Edit button?

## Section 3: Thank-You Notes (8 minutes)

Let's see how you can log and track thank-you notes to church members.

### Step 7: Global Thank-You Notes List

1. Click **"Thank-You Notes"** in the top menu
2. You should see a table with recent thank-you notes

**What to check:**
- Can you see who each note was sent to?
- Do you see the **channel** (Card, Email, Call, etc.)?
- Are some notes linked to donations or events?

### Step 8: View a Person's Profile

1. Click on a **person's name** in the thank-you notes list
   - Try clicking "John Smith" or "Robert Johnson"
2. You'll be taken to that person's profile page

**What to check:**
- Can you see their contact information?
- Do you see a "Thank-You Notes" section on their page?

### Step 9: Log a New Thank-You Note (Person)

1. On the person's profile page, click **"Log Thank-You Note"**
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Date:** Today's date (should be pre-filled)
   - **Channel:** Choose "Email"
   - **Subject:** "Thanks for your help with setup"
   - **Message:** "Just wanted to say thanks for helping set up the fellowship hall last week!"
3. Click **"Save Thank-You Note"**

**What to check:**
- Did the form appear when you clicked the button?
- Was it easy to fill out?
- After saving, did the note appear in the list on this page?

### Step 10: Go Back to Global Thank-You Notes

1. Click **"Thank-You Notes"** in the top menu again
2. Look for the note you just created

**What to check:**
- Can you see your new thank-you note in the global list?
- Does it show the person, channel, and subject?

## Section 4: Donor & Event Follow-Up (6 minutes)

Let's see how thank-you notes work with donations and events.

### Step 11: Donation Thank-You

1. Click **"Donations"** in the top menu (if you don't see it, you might not have the right role - that's OK, skip to Step 13)
2. Click on any recent donation (look for amounts like $100, $250, etc.)
3. Scroll down to the **"Thank-You Notes"** section

**What to check:**
- Can you see if a thank-you note was already sent for this donation?
- Is there a button to log a thank-you note?

### Step 12: Log a Donation Thank-You

1. Click **"Log Thank-You for Donation"**
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Date:** Today
   - **Channel:** Card
   - **Subject:** "Thank you for your generous gift"
   - **Message:** "Your support helps us continue our ministry!"
3. Save it

**What to check:**
- Did the form know who the donor was automatically?
- After saving, does the note appear on this donation page?

### Step 13: Event Thank-You

1. Click **"Events"** in the top menu
2. Click on the **"Church Workday"** event (or similar past event)
3. Scroll to the **"Thank-You Notes"** section

**What to check:**
- Can you see any notes logged for event volunteers?

### Step 14: Log an Event Thank-You

1. Click **"Log Thank-You for Event"**
2. **Select a person** from the dropdown (choose anyone)
3. Fill out:
   - **Channel:** In-Person
   - **Subject:** "Thanks for serving"
   - **Message:** "Appreciate your help at the workday!"
4. Save it

**What to check:**
- Was the person dropdown easy to use?
- Did the note save successfully?
